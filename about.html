TechTub services are professional services designed to facilitate the use of technology by enterprises and end users. TechTub services provide specialized technology-oriented solutions by combining the processes and functions of software, hardware, networks, telecommunications and electronics. TechTub services are also known as information technology services (ITS). In the IT industry, TechTub services are delivered according to business or enterprise requirements. Services range from basic Internet connectivity to enterprise application (EA) software. TechTub service providers include Internet Service Providers (ISP), Application Service Providers (ASP), cloud providers and developers.

  • Software development, integration and maintenance
  • Hardware
  • Networking integration, management and maintenance
  • Information security (IS)
  • IT management consultants
  • Mobile services
  • Web applications
